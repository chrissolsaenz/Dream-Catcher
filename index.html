Dream-catcher

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Don't you hate when you can't remember your dreams? Dream Catcher is an app where you can easily track, analyze and record you dreams, so you never forget a dream again! Dream catcher allows you to sort and customize your dream logs as well as set a mood for that dream. You also have the ability to set a lock for entry into your dream catcher, so you never have to worry about someone seeing your journal
